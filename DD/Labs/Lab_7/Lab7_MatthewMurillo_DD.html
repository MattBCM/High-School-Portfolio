What is a favicon?

A favicon is a little image in the tab of the website that represents a website. it is also knows as the tab icon or the shortcut icon. The purpose of this is to show the logo of the website to the company or to represent you. Also favicons are useful, because if you have so many tabs open, the favicon can let you know what website the tab is. So favicons are really useful tools when creating websites and should be necessary if you have your own logo.

How are favicons made?

favicons are made with a 16x16 pixel image. The image could be a JPG file, PNG file, even GIF file, but the size of it must be 16x16 pixels. each pixel is made up of a byte, and one byte is 8 bits of binary. In order to change the color of the pixel, you would use the RGB system. For each three bits in the binary in the pixel, it changes the intensity of red, blue and green. if you mix the colors, you can create others colors that might be necessary for your favicon. After coloring all of your pixels, you save the image the way you wanted like JPG or PNG and thats how favicons are created.

How did I create my favicon

I first had to think of an idea on what my favican had to be, so I was thinking of a big M on the middle of a currency. So I decided to make a red M on a yellow coin and then I started thinking of other things I could add. Then I decided to add a blue "halo" on top of the M, but then I started to think of other ways to improve. I then add a black curve that connect the end points of M and as I look at the favicon, I realized it look like a person wearing a helmet. it actually look to me like a trojan warrior, so I decided to keep it that way. So that was my favicon
