Director: Tony Leondis

Rated: PG

Length: 1 hr 27 min

Sarring: T.J. Miller, James Carden and Anna Faris

Emoji Movie is about an emoji named Gene who is very different from the other emojis. Each emoji has their own expression, but Gene doesn't. Gene decides to go embark on a quest with his friend Hi-5 to make himself "normal". Along the way, he met a hacker named Jailbreak and she knows how to reprogram Gene to be a normal "Meh" emoji. Along the journey, the phone that their world is in was about to be wiped out completely, so they had to rush to Dropbox quickly. At the climax of the Movie, Gene has feelings for Jailbreak, but she doesn't. This made Gene depressed and become a "Meh" emoji. Jailbreak realizes the consequence of her action and went back to the phone to tell Gene about himself. At the end, Jailbreak tells Gene to be himself and he becomes a multi-facial emoji, which stopped the boy from wiping his phone. All the emojis cheered for joy and Gene had the job he always dreamed of.
