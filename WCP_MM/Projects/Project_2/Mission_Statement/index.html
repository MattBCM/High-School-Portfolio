About Us

An idea sparked in the minds of four random and socially awkward teens with a passion of fashion, business, and web development started this company in a park in Elmhurst. These passions lead to our development. We wanted to combine our separate talents into one company to benefit the world’s fashion.

Mission Statement

Our mission from day one was to give the people a set of clothing that would adjust to their day. You could say that our clothing is the offspring of athleisure and formality. Best case scenario, we would wish to have clothing created to last the whole day, regardless of what activities you have to do. Just imagine how much money you could save if you only had to purchase one set of clothes for all of your activities!
