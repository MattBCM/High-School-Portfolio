What is MBI?

The Majin Buu Industries brings you the MBI Haptic Suit. This suit was mainly made for truly immersive game experience, yet it can also be used for military and medical reasons. You can feel everything in this suit, if you play in a first-person-shooter, you can feel where a bullet hits you, if you play in a cold climate, you can feel the coolness on your body from the suit. When the user puts this suit on, any action or movement will get recorded and utilized within the game. If they move their hand, their character's hand moves. This immersive experience will allow any user to feel like they are genuinely in the game.

How does it work?

The Majin Buu Industries MBI Haptic Suit has a complex algorithmic system that takes every single movement of the user, records it, and translates it to the movement of the character. The suit applies force, pressure, or resistance by using electronic actuators, pneumatics, and hydraulics. The entire exoskeleton can extend to the entire body, applying pressure, force, and resistance to the entire body, simulating any form of contact, explosion, punch, bullet, and so forth. For example, holding a ball in-game will trigger air bladders and motors to harden and constrict the user's hand to genuinely make it feel like they are holding a ball.
